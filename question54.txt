Question:
what should i write in a motivational letter for an internship?

Answer:

A good motivation letter for an internship is not about telling your whole life story. It should clearly answer four questions:

Why this internship?

Why you?

What skills/experience do you bring?

What do you want to gain and contribute?

Below is a clear structure, followed by a strong example tailored for a scientific/technical internship (which fits your background).
